# Red-Team Review Checklist

## Availability

- Are key player statuses sourced and timestamped?
- Are fitness reports official, media-sourced, or inferred?
- Are any players returning from injury with minutes risk?
- Are yellow-card suspension risks relevant?
- Is there late squad replacement risk?

## Tactical Matchup

- Can the favorite progress the ball under the opponent's press?
- Is either side vulnerable defending transitions?
- Is there a mismatch on wide channels, aerial duels, set pieces, or second balls?
- Does the opponent force the favored team into its weaker attacking pattern?
- Does the coach have a credible plan B?

## Data Quality

- Is recent form based on weak opposition?
- Are friendly matches overweighted?
- Are club metrics being transferred to national-team roles without context?
- Are xG, shot quality, and chance prevention aligned with results?
- Is the sample too small for the stated confidence?

## Tournament Context

- Could group-third qualification alter incentives?
- Is either team likely to rotate?
- Are travel distance, rest days, weather, altitude, or time zone materially different?
- Is bracket path affecting risk appetite?
- Does the venue create a home, semi-home, or hostile crowd effect?

## Prediction Calibration

- Does the stated confidence match the evidence?
- Are probabilities overly precise?
- Is the draw underweighted?
- Are low-scoring variance and set pieces considered?
- Are goalkeeper variance and penalty-shootout variance separated from 90-minute strength?
